Schick

Schick is a classic American razor brand with 100 years of history, but no unique voice. Partners & Spade completed a total brand re-haul including: logo, voice/visual identity, packaging, web, and a new campaign. This new identity and campaign repositions Schick away from the cliches of the category to celebrate individuality.
